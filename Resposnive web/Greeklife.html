Photo's of Gramma and Ginga

Great Grandma and Ginga pose for the camera in Gramma's house in Clarksburg, West Virginia. Even though they fight, curse and argue with each other; they still love one another and have been best friends their whole lives.
Ginga holds up a pendant she recieved at an award ceremony. She is 99 and still volounteers at hospitals and says "I love to walk down the halls and make people laugh and smile."
Gramma sits in her rocking chair holding flowers she recieved for her 104th birthday!
Gramma sits in her rocking chair again holding the hundreds of birthday cards from fans from across the world. These cards ranged from the USA, Canada, Brazil, China, Korea, Indonesia, The Phillipines and many more.
Gramma and Ginga eating at their favorite restaraunt- Twin Oaks. Their favorite thing to eat there is the salad and the Hogies!
Ginga (left) stands with both of her sisters, Gramma (middle) and Beebee in the early 1960's.
The Davisson Brothers Band dropped by to visit Gramma and Ginga.
