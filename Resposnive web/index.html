The University of Miami is a very great place to be and to go to school. It has a lot of fun and interesting things to do during the school day and during the weekends. The University of Miami is one of the nicest places to go to school in the country. It has great sports teams, great Greek Life and an all around awesome community.

School

The University of Miami is a private universtiy located in Coral Gables, Florida. It is about 30 minutes away from South Beach. The University of Miami has a little more than 17,000 students undergrad. The school is made up of 11 differrent schools and 180 different majors. It ranked number 46 best colleges in the USA.

Alumni

The University of Miami has a very close relationship with those who have graduated in the past. They try to stay as connected as possible to help current students get affiliated with good internships and jobs once they are out of college and in the real world. The university also has the Toppel Career center which helps many students have meetings and job interviews set up so that they can get a head start to their early career.

Popular Sports at the University of Miami

  • Basketball
  • Football
  • Lacrosse
  • Baseball
  • Swimming and Diving

The three most popular sports in Miami are football, basketball and baseball. These three sports are the best that the school has and the most popular for other students and alumni to watch. The football team this year had an almost perfect season, but were 10-0 at one point, which was one of the best records in College Football. Miami basketball and baseball are also super popular among students because they are both very good teams and win a good amount of the time.
