School

The University of Miami is a private universtiy located in Coral Gables, Florida. It is about 30 minutes away from South Beach. The University of Miami has a little more than 17,000 students undergrad. The school is made up of 11 differrent schools and 180 different majors. It ranked number 46 best colleges in the USA.
